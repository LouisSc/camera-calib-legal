Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

Louis Schestak ("I", "me", "the developer") built the Camera Calibration app (the "App") as an ad-supported application. This page informs you about my policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the App.

Key Point: All image processing and calibration data stays on your device. No images or calibration results are uploaded to any server.

1. Information Collection and Use

1.1 Data That Stays on Your Device (Not Collected by Me)

The following data is created and stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to me or any third-party server:

  • Camera Images: Photos captured or imported for calibration purposes are stored locally in the app's private storage.
  • Calibration Data: Camera matrices, distortion coefficients, detected corner points, and other calibration results.
  • App Settings: Language preferences, display calibration values, UI preferences.
  • Device Model Information: Your device manufacturer and model name are read locally to assist with display calibration. This information is not transmitted.

1.2 Data Collected by Third-Party Services

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain data as described in Section 4.

2. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

Permission Purpose Required
Camera Capture images of calibration patterns (e.g., checkerboards) for camera calibration Optional
Photo Library / Storage (Read) Import existing images from your gallery for calibration Optional
Storage (Write) Save calibration images and export calibration data Optional
Internet Load and display advertisements (Google AdMob) Yes (for ads)
Network State Check network availability for ad loading Yes (for ads)

Camera and storage permissions are only requested when you actively use the corresponding features. You can deny these permissions and still use other parts of the App.

3. Image Processing

All image processing, including checkerboard pattern detection and camera calibration computations, is performed entirely on your device using the OpenCV library. Specifically:

  • No images are uploaded to any server.
  • No image data is shared with any third party.
  • Images are stored in the App's private directory and are not accessible by other apps.
  • You can delete all stored images at any time from within the App.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. The App requests non-personalized ads by default.

4.1 Data Collected by Google AdMob

Google AdMob may collect and process the following data:

  • IP address
  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID)
  • General device information (OS version, device model, screen size)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

For details on how Google processes this data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy and How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps.

4.2 GDPR Consent (European Economic Area)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the App will display a consent dialog (powered by Google's User Messaging Platform) before loading any ads. You can:

  • Grant or deny consent for personalized or non-personalized advertising
  • Change your consent preferences at any time via Settings > Privacy Settings > Manage Ad Consent within the App

4.3 Ad-Free Option

The App may offer an option to remove ads through an in-app purchase. When ads are disabled, no ad-related data is collected or transmitted.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All app data (images, calibration results, settings) is stored locally on your device in the App's private storage area.
  • No data is stored on external servers by me.
  • All network communication (for ads) uses encrypted HTTPS connections.
  • You can delete all locally stored data at any time through the App's settings or by uninstalling the App.

6. Data Export and Sharing

The App allows you to export calibration results in various formats (JSON, CSV, OpenCV YAML/XML, MATLAB). When you use the export or share function:

  • The exported file is created locally on your device.
  • Sharing is handled through your device's native sharing mechanism (e.g., Android ShareSheet, iOS Share Sheet).
  • The developer has no access to exported or shared data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by tagging ad requests appropriately. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data through this App, please contact me so I can take appropriate action.

8. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

Service Purpose Privacy Policy
Google AdMob Advertising Google Privacy Policy
Google UMP SDK GDPR Consent Management Google Privacy Policy

The App does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Individual images: Delete specific calibration images within the App.
  • All calibration data: Use the App's option to delete all calibration images and data.
  • All app data: Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.
  • Ad consent: Reset your advertising identifier through your device's system settings.

10. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights regarding data processed by third-party ad services:

  • Right of Access: You can request information about data collected through ads.
  • Right to Erasure: You can reset your advertising identifier or withdraw consent.
  • Right to Object: You can opt out of personalized advertising through the consent dialog or device settings.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: You can change your ad consent at any time via Settings > Privacy Settings in the App.

Since I do not collect or store any personal data on my servers, most data subject requests should be directed to Google regarding ad-related data.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
